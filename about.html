Anime Summary

Genre: Supernatural, Action, Drama

Created by: Jun Maeda

Studio: P.A. Works, Aniplex

Episodes: 13

Release Date: July 4, 2015 - September 27, 2015

Synopsis: In a world where a small percentage of teenagers develop supernatural abilities, Yuu Otosaka has the power to temporarily possess others. However, he must confront the consequences of his actions and learn to use his powers for good while attending Hoshinoumi Academy, a school for gifted teens.
